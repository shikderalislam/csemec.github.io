About Us

Mymensingh Engineering College commonly referred to as MEC is a public undergraduate college in Mymensingh, Bangladesh, established in 2007. MEC is an elite institution for graduating in engineering disciplines in Bangladesh, and is affiliated with the University of Dhaka under the Faculty of Engineering & Technology. It is the modern engineering colleges in Bangladesh. Postgraduate studies and more Engineering departments are going to be introduced soon. Mymensingh Engineering College is located about 7 kilometers away from the main city of Mymensingh.
The Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) of MEC offers Bachelor Degree in Computer Science and Engineering. The Education of CSE MEC is world class in both the Curricula and Research activity. The best students from all over Bangladesh join this prestigious department. The highly competitive environment, world-class facilities and the hard-working faculties nourish every student to be a prime of the field. Currently there are about 180 students studying in Undergraduate Program .
